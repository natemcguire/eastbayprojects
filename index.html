Things to Know

25Y Rule (FMVSS)

Vehicles (including motorcycles) 25+ years old are exempt from DOT safety regs (federal motor vehicle safety standards). (Before )

21Y Rule (EPA)

Vehicles 21+ years old are exempt from EPA emissions compliance. (Before )

<25Y Rule

Must meet U.S. DOT + EPA standards or be imported under "Show & Display" (rare, restrictive).

25Y Date

Clock starts from month of manufacture, not model year.

State Regs Still Apply

Even if federally legal, state laws (e.g., California CARB) may block registration.

Import Duty

~2.5% for cars, 25% for trucks, when bringing into the U.S.

Customs Form

HS-7 (DOT) + 3520-1 (EPA) filed at entry.

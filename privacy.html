Privacy Notice

This notice explains how East Bay Projects handles information submitted through eastbayprojects.com.

Information we collect

When you submit a project inquiry, we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, organization, website, budget range, timeline, and project description. We may also record referral and advertising attribution information, including UTM parameters and Google click identifiers.

How we use information

We use this information to evaluate and respond to inquiries, prepare project recommendations, operate our business, prevent abuse, and understand which outreach and advertising produces useful inquiries.

Sharing and retention

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that operate our website, infrastructure, analytics, or advertising, including Cloudflare and Google. We retain inquiries for as long as reasonably useful for business, security, and recordkeeping purposes, then delete or anonymize them.

Advertising and measurement

We may use Google Ads or analytics tools to measure visits and form submissions. These services may use cookies or similar technologies subject to their own policies and your browser settings.

Your choices

You may ask us to correct or delete information you submitted, subject to any legal or recordkeeping obligations. Contact contact@eastbayprojects.com.
